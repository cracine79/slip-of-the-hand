Rules & Classifications of SlotHs


Slips of the Hand (SlotHs) occur when one or both hands are mispositioned on the keyboard.

SlotHs are categorized into three groups: Half SlotHs, True SlotHs, and Wild SlotHs

SlotHs can be shorter, but not longer than the original SlotH generating word.

Welcome to Slip of the Hand!


A Slip of the hand occurs when you type a word or phrase with one or both hands in the incorrect position, and another word or phrase is typed as a result!


For example, imagine the hands below are trying to type the word DRY, but started one key to the left. This would result in accientally typing the word SET!


Go ahead, give it a try! Enter a word of phrase and see what slips of the hand you could have had!
